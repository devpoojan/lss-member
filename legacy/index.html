About the Samiti

શ્રી લાલાબાપા સેવા સમિતિ એ સામાજિક અને માનવીય સેવાના ઉમદા હેતુથી સ્થાપિત સંસ્થા છે.

Shree Lalabapa Seva Samiti is a dedicated charitable trust focused on uplifting the community through various social and humanitarian activities. Inspired by the teachings of Shree Lalabapa, we strive to bring positive change to the lives of people in Vadaj and beyond.

Our mission is to serve all living beings with compassion, following the divine path of "Seva" (service).

Our Seva Activities

અન્નક્ષેત્ર (Food Distribution)

Providing nutritious meals to the needy and underprivileged sections of society regularly.

શિક્ષણ સેવા (Education)

Supporting children's education through supplies, scholarships, and guidance for a brighter future.

આરોગ્ય સેવા (Health)

Organizing blood donation camps and health checkups for the well-being of our community.
